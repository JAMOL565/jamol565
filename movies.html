My favorite movies

UDAROV

Los Angeles, California is a world of endless opportunities during the day and unreal Hollywood parties at night. Both of these worlds seeks to conquer a young DJ named Cole, who is tirelessly working on his punching track, which should blow up the whole world. Experienced producer James takes notice and takes him under his wing, but things get extremely confusing when Cole falls in love with his young girlfriend, James. Soon Cole faces a difficult choice between love, loyalty, old friends and the future he dreams of.

IGRI RAZUMA

From world fame to the sinful depths - John Forbes Nash Jr. learned all this the hard way. A mathematical genius, at the dawn of his career, he made a titanic work in the field of game theory, which turned this section of mathematics and practically brought him international fame. However, literally at the same time arrogant and successful in women, Nash gets the blow of fate, which turns his own life.

FORSAJ

Conquered tops once ceased to matter, because now the legendary gang of riders waiting for the new, the most serious of all the tests! From now on, they will have to demonstrate their skills in the vastness of an indecently expensive Arabic city. The beauty of landscapes, incredibly valuable cars, mad speed and a cunning adversary that owns a multi-million dollar state. Is it given into the hands without a fierce struggle? Perhaps this race will be the last for many, but there is a chance that the fearless heroes will still be able to overcome such an unreal barrier.

HITMAN

Agent 47 is hired to assassinate Russian President Belikov. The task was completed successfully: a sniper bullet from a skyscraper under construction accurately hits the target. However, payment is delayed: a mysterious customer demands the murder of a witness - a Russian prostitute. Unfortunately, this is just an excuse to kill the killer and cover his tracks. Meanwhile, the media reported that Belikov was only injured. Hitman understands that this is a double. Agent 47 is trying to reach customers and bring the mission to the end, killing the twin of the president. The life and movement of the killer is complicated by both Interpol and the FSB.
